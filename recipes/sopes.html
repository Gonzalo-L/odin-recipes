Sopes

Photo Credits: Dahyana Yasada R. R., CC BY-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Sopes is a Mexican dish that consists of a fried corn dough shell that is covered in toppings. The toppings can vary but ultimately consist of sour cream, cotija cheese, chorizo, onions, lettuce and salsa. Some people choose to replace the chorizo with beans for a meatless option. The sides of a sope are pinched up making it resemble a bowl.

Ingredients

  • Sope base
  • Sour cream
  • Cotija cheese(or other cheese type)
  • Chorizo(or other meat type)
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
