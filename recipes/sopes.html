Sopes

Photo Credits: Dahyana Yasada R. R., CC BY-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Sopes is a Mexican dish that consists of a fried corn dough shell that is covered in toppings. The toppings can vary but ultimately consist of sour cream, cotija cheese, chorizo, onions, lettuce and salsa. Some people choose to replace the chorizo with beans for a meatless option. The sides of a sope are pinched up making it resemble a bowl.

Ingredients

  • Sope base
  • Sour cream
  • Cotija cheese (or other cheese type)
  • Chorizo (or other meat type)
  • 1 Onion
  • Lettuce
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 3 Serrano peppers
  • Salt

Steps

For Sopes

  1. Chop onion and lettuce.
  2. Remove casing from chorizo.
  3. Place chorizo in skillet and cook until color changes.
  4. Fry sopes until golden brown.
  5. Place sopes on a plate.
  6. Remove excess oil from sopes with paper towel.
  7. Apply sour cream to sope base.
  8. Put chorizo ontop of cream. Followed by the chooped onion, lettuce and cheese.
  9. Finally cover in salsa for spice!!

For Salsa

  1. Boil tomatoes and peppers until soft.
  2. Place tomatoes and peppers in blender.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and blend.
